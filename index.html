Who We Are

Ibraheem Industries is a leading import-export company based in Karachi, Pakistan, specializing in premium agricultural commodities. Established in 2006 and headquartered at Plot No. 56-B, Landhi Industrial Zone, we have built a reputation for excellence in international trade of Sugar, Flour, Edible Oils, and Rice.

With over two decades of experience, we serve as a vital bridge connecting Pakistan's agricultural wealth with global markets while also importing quality products to meet domestic demand. Our dual expertise in both import and export operations ensures comprehensive supply chain solutions for our international partners.

Sourcing from the fertile plains of Punjab and Sindh, we export exceptional quality commodities to discerning buyers across the Middle East, Africa, Europe, and Asia. Simultaneously, our import operations bring world-class products to Pakistani markets, supporting local industries and consumers.

We combine traditional trading expertise with modern supply chain management, ensuring every shipment—whether inbound or outbound—meets international food safety standards and exceeds client expectations. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and timely delivery has made us a trusted partner in global agricultural trade.
