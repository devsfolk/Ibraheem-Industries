Where We Source

Pakistan's most fertile agricultural regions supply our premium products

Punjab Province

The breadbasket of Pakistan, Punjab's irrigated plains produce 80% of the country's wheat and a significant portion of Basmati rice. We work with farmer cooperatives in:

  • Faisalabad - High-protein milling wheat
  • Lahore & Sheikhupura - Premium Basmati rice
  • Multan - Wheat and cotton seed oil
  • Gujranwala - Aromatic 1121 Basmati

Sindh Province

Sindh's fertile Indus River delta creates ideal conditions for rice cultivation and sugarcane. Our sourcing network includes:

  • Larkana & Shikarpur - Irri-6 and Irri-9 rice
  • Thatta & Badin - Non-Basmati export varieties
  • Sanghar - Sugar mills and refined sugar
  • Nawabshah - Rice processing hubs
