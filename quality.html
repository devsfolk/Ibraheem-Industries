Our Quality Commitment

At Ibraheem Industries, quality is not just a checkpoint—it's woven into every stage of our operations. From selecting seeds to final shipment, we maintain rigorous standards that meet and exceed international requirements.

Pre-Procurement Inspection

Field visits and farmer audits before harvest. We evaluate soil health, irrigation practices, and pest management to ensure quality from the ground up.

Laboratory Testing

State-of-the-art labs test every batch for moisture, contamination, purity, aflatoxin, pesticide residues, and microbiological safety.

Final Pre-Shipment Audit

Independent third-party verification before cargo leaves our facilities. Complete documentation and compliance with destination country regulations.

Laboratory Testing Standards

Comprehensive testing protocols for every product category

Physical & Chemical Tests

  • Moisture Content Analysis:

    Ensures optimal dryness for storage and prevents mold growth

  • Protein & Gluten Testing:

    Determines nutritional value and baking quality for wheat/flour

  • Foreign Matter Detection:

    Screening for stones, dust, straw, and other contaminants

  • Broken Grain Percentage:

    Quality grading based on whole grain integrity

  • Color & Whiteness:

    Visual quality assessment using Lovibond and spectrophotometry

Safety & Contamination Tests

  • Aflatoxin Testing:

    Detects harmful mycotoxins; kept below 15 ppb for export

  • Pesticide Residue Analysis:

    Multi-residue screening per EU and FDA limits

  • Heavy Metals Detection:

    Testing for lead, cadmium, mercury, and arsenic

  • Microbiological Analysis:

    Checks for E. coli, Salmonella, total plate count, and coliforms

  • Peroxide & Free Fatty Acids (Oils):

    Measures freshness and stability of edible oils

100% Batch Testing Guarantee

Every lot is laboratory-tested before release. We provide detailed Certificate of Analysis (CoA) with every shipment, showing exact test results and compliance confirmation.
