Trade Terms & Pricing Structure

FOB (Free on Board)

Sugar loaded on vessel at origin port. Buyer arranges and pays for ocean freight and insurance. Common for Brazil, Thailand shipments.

CIF (Cost, Insurance, Freight)

Complete delivery to destination port including freight and insurance. Most preferred term for import operations to Pakistan and export to regional markets.

CFR (Cost and Freight)

Similar to CIF but insurance arranged by buyer. Competitive pricing option for experienced importers with own insurance arrangements.

Export Documentation

  • Commercial Invoice
  • Packing List
  • Bill of Lading (B/L)
  • Certificate of Origin
  • Health/Phytosanitary Certificate
  • SGS/Intertek Quality Inspection Report
  • Halal Certificate (if required)
  • Fumigation Certificate

Quality Inspection Protocol

  • Pre-shipment Inspection: SGS/Intertek lab testing at origin
  • Parameters Tested: ICUMSA color, pol%, moisture, ash, granulation
  • Food Safety: Microbiological testing, heavy metals, pesticide residues
  • Certification: ISO 9001, HACCP, Halal, Kosher compliance
  • Container Inspection: Verification of cleanliness and stuffing
