# Ibraheem Industries - Premium Food Export Website

A modern, professional, and responsive multi-page website for Ibraheem Industries, a leading import/export company based in Pakistan specializing in premium agricultural commodities.

## 🌟 Features

### Design & Styling
- **Color Scheme**: 
  - Primary: Deep Emerald/Agri Green (emerald-800) - representing agriculture and Pakistan
  - Secondary: Premium Metallic Gold/Amber (amber-500) - representing quality
- **Typography**: Inter font family with clean, corporate styling
- **Responsive Design**: Fully mobile-responsive with burger menu
- **Premium Look**: Modern cards, hover effects, smooth transitions, and high-quality imagery

### Pages Included

1. **Home Page (`index.html`)**
   - Hero section with impactful tagline and CTAs
   - Company overview
   - Featured products grid with hover effects
   - Global reach section
   - Statistics counter (15K+ tons exported, 35 countries, 20 years, 500+ clients)
   - Why Choose Us section

2. **About Us (`about.html`)**
   - Company history and story
   - Vision and mission statements
   - Message from the CEO/Director
   - Core values (Integrity, Quality, Sustainability, Partnership, Innovation, Community)
   - Leadership team profiles

3. **Products Catalog (`products.html`)**
   - Interactive tabbed interface for easy navigation
   - Detailed product categories:
     - **Rice**: Basmati (Super Kernel, 1121) and Non-Basmati varieties with specifications
     - **Wheat**: Premium milling wheat and refined flour with protein/gluten content
     - **Edible Oils**: Sunflower, Canola, Palm oil with quality standards
     - **Sugar**: Refined white and brown sugar with ICUMSA ratings
   - Specifications, packaging details, and inquiry buttons

4. **Operations (`operations.html`)**
   - Complete supply chain breakdown (Sourcing → Quality Control → Processing → Shipping)
   - Sourcing regions (Punjab, Sindh)
   - Processing facilities details
   - Global logistics network
   - Export documentation process

5. **Quality Assurance (`quality.html`)**
   - International certifications (ISO 9001:2015, HACCP, Halal, FDA)
   - Laboratory testing procedures
   - Quality control processes
   - Hygiene and safety standards
   - Traceability systems

6. **Sustainability (`sustainability.html`)**
   - Environmental stewardship initiatives
   - Fair trade practices
   - Support for 3,000+ local farmers
   - Eco-friendly farming methods
   - Community development programs

7. **Media & Insights (`media.html`)**
   - Latest news and company updates
   - Market trends and commodity insights
   - Industry developments in Pakistani agriculture

8. **Contact (`contact.html`)**
   - Professional contact form with validation
   - Company contact information (Karachi office)
   - Google Maps integration
   - Business hours and social media links

### Interactive Features (`script.js`)

✅ **Mobile Menu Toggle** - Responsive navigation with smooth open/close
✅ **Smooth Scrolling** - Enhanced user experience for anchor links
✅ **Product Tab Switching** - Dynamic content display on products page
✅ **Counter Animation** - Animated statistics using Intersection Observer
✅ **Active Nav Highlighting** - Current page indication
✅ **Form Validation** - Contact form with data handling
✅ **Scroll to Top Button** - Convenient navigation for long pages
✅ **Hover Effects** - Enhanced interactivity on cards and elements

## 🚀 Technology Stack

   - No build process required
